Tomato pasta bake

Make this simple student pasta and tomato dish for a delicious, filling and budget-friendly supper. it is to use up simple storecupboard ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 400g can cherry or chopped tomatos
  • pinch sugar

Preparation

  1. Heat the oil in a medium pan and fry the onion for 5 mins until softend and lightly golden.
  2. Meanwhile, cook the pasta according to pack instruction. Heat the grill to high. Mix together the cheese and bresdcrumbs.
  3. Drain the pasta and stir into the sauce. Tip in the Frankfurters and spoon into a heatproof dish.
